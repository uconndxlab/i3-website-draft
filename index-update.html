WHAT IS i3?

The Internal Insights & Innovation (i3) team provides custom software development, web design, and other digital services in support of improving UConn's business processes, academic operations, and research enterprise.

We're not trying to reinvent the wheel— sometimes, we just help our colleagues find the wheel and use it, making the most of the tools already available at UConn. But when the wheel doesn't fit the task, we step in with a lean, agile approach—building, testing, and refining solutions that adapt to the university's evolving needs.

Design & Development

Web

Nexus

Nexus, a University of Connecticut engagement and retention platform, places actionable data in the hands of the advising community to foster student success. Tools include dashboards, appointment scheduling, note sharing, event registration, and more.

Visit Nexus

Mobile

WellSCAN

Built in collaboration with the UConn Rudd Center for Food Policy & Health, WellSCAN is a website and mobile-friendly scanning application made to help food banks and food pantries better identify, rank, and log the nutritional quality of their inventory, based on the HER guidelines.

Visit WellSCAN

Software

Research Insights for Faculty

RIF is a website that provides UConn faculty and staff with access to financial reports on their sponsored project accounts. Powered by the data from OVPR, faculty and staff can view transactions, comment and review on monthly reports, and even project through the account's lifespan.
