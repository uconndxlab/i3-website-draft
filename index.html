What is [i3]?

At i3, we create custom software and web solutions while maximizing the use of existing resources to support the university's business processes, academic operations, and research initiatives.

We we're not trying to reinvent the wheel— sometimes, we just help our colleagues find the wheel and use it, making the most of the tools already available at UConn. But when the wheel doesn't fit the task, we step in with a lean, agile approach—building, testing, and refining solutions that adapt to the university's evolving needs.

What We Do

For UConn and Beyond

Cubed Labs (❒ labs)

We partner with UConn administration and departments to enhance their systems through tailored digital solutions. Whether developing custom tools or optimizing existing resources, our goal is to provide digital services that simplify and improve the experience for UConn faculty, staff, and students.

  • KFS-friendly Internal Billing

Digital Experience Group

I3 operates a Digital Services Core (the Digital Experience Group), providing the UConn Research Community with access to app development, web design, UI/UX design, and other innovative technology services, helping researchers bring their grant funded projects to life with cutting-edge digital solutions.

CMOT

CMOT

Web Development UX Design

What if classroom evaluators had a tablet app for their in-class assessment? We built CMOT (Classroom Management Observation Tool) under the direction of Dr. Jen Freeman of the UConn Neag School of Education and her associated UConn REP (Research Excellence Program) grant. It allows observers to enter progress monitoring items, which have been validated for informing decisions about relative strengths/needs with positive and proactive classroom management. It also contains a checklist of empirically-supported practice features to periodically "look for".
